What do we do?

  • Community:
    • As a community first organization we will aim to host bonding events for our members.
    • As we continue to grow and develop these events will as well. We may start by reserving rooms for us to meet, catch up, bond, and just have a space for students of alike backgrounds and experiences to interact.
    • As time goes on, our numbers grow both physically and fiscally, these nights may grow to provide a space for opportunities to embrace culture through food, art (like film, literature, etc).
    • Maybe we take trips to go see different art pieces in the community that connect to our identities.
    • Possibilites are endless.
  • Theatre Board:
    • As a new board/org we have very little funds. Therefore while this spring our focus will be building a community in line with the foundation for this group, we will spend the summer figuring out fundraising opportunities through small showcases and cabarets organized by the team that will be opportunities to feature all of our members talents both on stage and off.
    • Spring: Our First Show
    • Aim to do year round seasons starting in 2023.

How to get involved!

  • Join us at our functions and events the rest of this year, and the years to come.
  • VC2 looks different than the other student boards, that means we want you to help.
    • Join us at our fundraising events, we are new and need help developing this board. We want to bring you everything we can, but that starts with that dough, please help us fundraise for this amazing organization.
    • We will have a few open board meetings for yall to join and see how our board operates. We do want to be transparent about our organization and find value in previewing our group to the community.
